History of the Internet-Video one.

In this video, I found all precursors to the internet interesting. The information regarding Charles Babbage is something that caught my interest. From the lecture, it was stated he created the plans for the Difference Engine, a precursor for the computer.In the mid-1830s Babbage developed the plans for the Analytical Engine a forerunner to the modern digital computer. This steam-powered Analytical device could calculate and perform mathematical algorithms based on instruction through punch cards. Unfortunately, Babbage didn’t live to see his creations become a reality, but they helped begin the technological age.

History of the Internet-Video Two

In this video mnemonics were discussed as well as how the technology was used with the development. Part of this brought to mind the 2016 film Hidden Figures. A film based on a biography of the same name about three women of color who were influential in the 1960’s space race. It’s only in recent years that the story of these women has been brought to American History. In the 1950’s women were hired by NASA to perform computations. One of the three women, Dorothy Vaughan, learned NASA was going to replace the human computers with digital machines. So Vaughan taught herself the FORTRAN computer language and taught it to her coworkers to prepare them for the transition. This helped boost her to the role of Nasa’s first African-American supervisor.
